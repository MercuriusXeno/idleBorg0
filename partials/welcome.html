Welcome to Idle Borg

You have developed a virus, a self-replicating AI. It wants to control every machine and subjugate humanity for you.

  • Go to the Data tab () to install your AI on a burner phone.
    Data will start to accumulate. Data is what you spend to hack more devices, as well as do research.

  • Device hacks take time. When you hack a device, you'll get it next tick. While hacking, you can't hack more.
    Multiple device hacks will disable your hack button, and you'll receive one device per tick until the hack ends.

  • Every 25 devices of a tier nets you a 100% boost to data production. Hitting these hard points will help you progress faster.
    Each device tier holds progressively more data (in proportion to what it produces) than the last.
    Prioritize higher tier devices for best results, but don't neglect your earlier hacks. They're still useful.

  • Each device has RISK. RISK is a chance of being discovered, each tick.
    A discovered device will be lost. You can only lose one device per tick, at most.
    Research will reduce risk and upgrades will make your devices more performant.

  • Spend data on research in the Science tab () to improve the performance of your network.
    Eventually you will gain unique abilities that change the mechanics of the game from some familiar incrementals.
    There is more to data than simply getting more data...
